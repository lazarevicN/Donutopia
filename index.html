WHO ARE WE?

Donutopia is place where the most delicious donuts in Serbia starts their journey! Donutopia was founded on 2th of May, 2017 by the group of people who were friends for 10 years and still counting. Their love for donuts made them to start searching, exploring and mixing flavors and recipes to make some of the best recipes for the most delicious donuts now! After years of searching, experimenting, mixing, making thousands of donuts, the time has come to open the store and start selling those delicious donuts. Therefore, life of the store of the most delicious donuts begins! It all started by giving store a name Donutopia! We combined Donuts and Utopia, as an imagined place or state of things in which everythings perfect, to describe our store, which is a place where everythings perfect starting from donuts to our team!
